White Horse

Horses have impressive long term memory. They can sleep both lying down and standing up.

  • Favorite Food: Hay. They like sweets.
  • Preferences: Horses like classical music.
  • Legs: They cannot get frostbites on their legs.
  • Water Drinking: They drink at least 25 gallons of water a day.

White horses are born white and stay white throughout their lives. White horses may have brown, blue, or hazel eyes. "True white" horses, especially those that carry one of the dominant white (W) genes, are rare. Many white horses have dark eyes, though some have blue eyes.
